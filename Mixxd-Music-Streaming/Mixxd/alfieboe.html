Alfie Boe

Alfie Boe is an award-winning English tenor best known for his standout performances in musical theatre, including his iconic role as Jean Valjean in *Les Misérables*. With a powerful operatic voice and a charismatic stage presence, he blends classical, theatrical, and contemporary styles, bringing emotional depth to every performance.
